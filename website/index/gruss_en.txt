The villa "Tannenhof" (Pine court) in Bad Tabarz was built in 1887 as a summer residence for a prosperous family and has seen many guests since then.
The house was renovated completely in 1999 and has now 2 vacation apartments for non-smokers fitted out comfortably.
On the property there are game possibilities for children, garden furnitures and game equipment, parking lots, table tennis, BBQ.
You can store bicycles and winter sport equipment here.
You can use the washing machine for a small fee.
