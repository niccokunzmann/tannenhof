You can reach us by car from the highway A4 (exit Waltershausen/Tabarz). 
After you left, turn south where you can see building vehicles and a crane. 
Turn right at the first traffic light. 
After 4km you drive straight at the trafficlight (you can see a gas station on your right).
After 1km turn right to enter Tabarz. 
Follow the street until you reach a square with a bank named "Sparkasse". 
Turn left. 
Then you can see the Villa "Tannenhof". 
Turn left again into "Alexandrinenweg".
